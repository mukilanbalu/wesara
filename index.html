WHO | WHAT IS SEED PAPER | WHY | HOW

Paper that grows and bloom
Invitations | promo cards | wrist bands | greetings

Plantable seed paper is special handmade, biodegradable,
eco-friendly paper. Made with post-consumer materials and
embedded with wildflower or other plant seeds. When the
paper is planted in soil, seeds will germinate and the paper
composts away. All that's left behind is colorful wildflowers
with beautiful memories.

WHO | WHAT | WHY | HOW TO PLANT IT

Step - 1

Make the used seed paper in to small pieces and dip them in water for 2-3 hours.

Step - 2

Place the paper on the soil(paper can be planted indoors or outside). Cover seed paper with 1/4 inch layer of soil and tamp down very gently.

Step - 3

Water it regularly (2-3 times) a day. For first 10 days Keep the paper moist at all times as the water is necessary for germination.

Step - 4

Once sprouts appear, continue to keep the paper moist but be careful not to overwater. Once sturdy plants appear, water as needed and keep them in a warm sunlight.
